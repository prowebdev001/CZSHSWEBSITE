TVL(Technical-Vocational-Livelihood)TRACK

ICT-PROGRAMMING

(Information and Communications Technology)

(Programming)

The ICT-Programming strand is a senior high school program that focuses on developing students' skills and knowledge in the field of information and communications technology (ICT) and computer programming. This strand is designed for students who are interested in pursuing careers in software development, game development, web development, and other related fields. The ICT-Programming strand curriculum includes courses that cover a range of topics such as programming languages, software development methodologies, web development, database management, and computer networks. Students learn how to design, develop, and test software applications, as well as how to analyze and solve technical problems. They also learn how to work with different types of computer systems and platforms.

ICT-CSS

(Information and Communications Technology)

(Computer System Servicing)

ICT-Computer System Servicing (CSS) is a senior high school strand under the Information and Communications Technology (ICT) track. It is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills to install, configure, maintain, and repair computer systems and networks. The CSS strand covers topics such as computer hardware and software, network protocols, programming languages, and database management. It also includes training in the use of tools and techniques for troubleshooting and problem-solving. Students in the CSS strand will learn how to assemble and disassemble computer hardware components, install operating systems and application software, set up network infrastructure, configure security protocols, and perform routine maintenance tasks. They will also learn programming concepts, such as variables, data types, loops, and functions.

H.E-COOKERY

(Home Economics-Cookery)

The H.E. Cookery strand is a program offered in the senior high school curriculum that focuses on developing students' knowledge and skills in the culinary arts. "H.E." stands for Home Economics, which encompasses a broad range of life skills related to managing households and families. The Cookery strand is designed to prepare students for careers in the food service industry, such as chefs, line cooks, and food service managers. The program provides students with a foundation in food science, safety, nutrition, and culinary techniques. Students learn how to prepare a variety of dishes, including appetizers, entrees, desserts, and baked goods. The Cookery strand also emphasizes hands-on learning, which allows students to practice and develop their culinary skills in a real-world setting. Students work in commercial kitchens, restaurants, and other food service establishments to gain experience and learn from industry professionals.

H.E-HOUSEKEEPING

(Home Economics-Housekeeping)

The H.E. (Home Economics) Housekeeping strand is a technical-vocational track offered in Senior High School in the Philippines. This strand focuses on providing students with the knowledge and skills needed to manage and maintain households, hotels, and other similar establishments. The Housekeeping strand is designed to develop students' competencies in the areas of housekeeping, food and beverage, laundry, and front office services.
