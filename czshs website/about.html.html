ABOUT

Cielito Zamora
Senior High School

      Cielito Zamora Senior High School (CZSHS) is a public secondary educational institution located in Cielito, Caloocan City. The school was founded in 2016 offering senior high school program. The school is a K-12 accredited school by the Department of Education (DepEd). It has Academic track such as Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), Science Technology Engineering Mathematics(STEM), and General Academic Strand as well as Technical-Vocational-Livelihood track specializes in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and Home Economics (HE) for its senior high school and as of now the said school also started to offer the Arts and Design Track. The school is consist of four buildings. The building 1, 2, and 3 are four story buildings and the other one which is the building 4 is a 2 story building which inludes the guidance office, registrar's office, and the principal's office. We invite you to visit our campus and to experience the warm welcoming of our loving teachers, students, and staffs.

MISSION

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where: Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment. Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner. Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen. Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners

VISION

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.
