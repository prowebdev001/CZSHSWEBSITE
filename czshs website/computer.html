COMPUTER LAB

      Welcome to the computer lab of Cielito Zamora Senior High School, where technology meets education in an innovative and dynamic environment. Our computer lab serves as a hub for students, providing them with the tools and resources necessary to excel in the digital age. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and internet access, our computer lab offers a modern and conducive space for learning, exploration, and collaboration. With a wide array of computers, laptops, and other digital devices, we ensure that every student has access to the necessary resources to enhance their digital literacy skills.

       Students can explore various software applications, design graphics, edit videos, develop websites, and engage in coding and programming projects. We strive to empower our students with the necessary digital skills to become lifelong learners and leaders in an increasingly interconnected world. Step into our computer lab, and embark on a transformative journey where technology opens doors to endless opportunities for growth, exploration, and success.
