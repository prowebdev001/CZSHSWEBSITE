ARTS AND DESIGN TRACK

PERFORMING ARTS

      The Arts and Design Track with a focus on Performing Arts Strand is an educational program that offers students a comprehensive curriculum in the field of performing arts. This track is designed for students who have a passion for the arts, particularly in the areas of dance, music, and theater. The Performing Arts Strand provides students with opportunities to develop their skills and talents in various performing arts disciplines.

       The Arts and Design Track with a focus on Performing Arts Strand provides students with a strong foundation in the performing arts, preparing them for further studies or careers in various fields such as theater, music, dance, film, television, and entertainment. By choosing the Arts and Design Track with a focus on Performing Arts Strand, students embark on a transformative educational journey that celebrates artistic expression, fosters artistic growth, and prepares them for a future filled with opportunities in the world of performing arts.
