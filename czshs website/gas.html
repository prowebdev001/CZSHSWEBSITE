ACADEMIC TRACK

GAS

(General Academic Strand)

      The General Academic Strand (GAS) is an academic track under the K-12 education system. It is designed to provide students with a well-rounded education that focuses on developing their critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. GAS offers a broad range of subjects across different disciplines, providing students with a solid foundation for higher education.

      One of the key advantages of the General Academic Strand is that it allows students to have more flexibility and freedom in choosing their future career paths.Students who opt for the GAS can pursue a wide range of college degree programs, including liberal arts, social sciences, natural sciences, and even some professional courses like law or medicine, depending on the university requirements. Students who complete the GAS are equipped with analytical thinking, research abilities, and effective communication skills, making them well-prepared for a variety of academic and professional pursuits.
