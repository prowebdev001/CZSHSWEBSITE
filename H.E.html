H.E.-FOOD AND BEVERAGE SERVICES

(Home Economics-Food and Beverage Services)

The H.E. (Home Economics) Food and Beverage Services strand is an educational program or specialization that focuses on developing knowledge and skills in the food and beverage industry. It is typically offered as part of a vocational or technical education curriculum, providing students with practical training and theoretical understanding of various aspects of food and beverage services. The H.E. Food and Beverage Services strand aims to prepare students for careers in the hospitality industry, particularly in restaurants, hotels, resorts, cafes, and other food service establishments. The program covers a wide range of topics related to food and beverage operations, customer service, culinary skills, and hospitality management.

H.E.-BREAD AND PASTRY PRODUCTION

(Home Economics-Bread and Pastry Production)

The H.E. Bread and Pastry Production strand refers to a specialized vocational or technical education program focused on developing skills in the art of bread and pastry making. "H.E." stands for Higher Education, indicating that this program is typically offered at the secondary or post-secondary level. This strand is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of various baking techniques, ingredients, and production processes involved in the creation of bread and pastry items. It aims to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills required for a successful career in the baking industry.

H.E.-COOKERY

(Home Economics-Cookery)

The H.E (Home Economics) Cookery strand refers to a specific area of study within the field of Home Economics, also known as Family and Consumer Sciences. Home Economics encompasses various aspects of everyday living, including food preparation, nutrition, household management, and personal finance. Cookery, in particular, focuses on developing culinary skills and knowledge related to cooking and food preparation. The H.E Cookery strand typically involves learning a wide range of cooking techniques, understanding ingredient selection and their nutritional value, and exploring different cuisines and food cultures. Students enrolled in this strand gain practical experience in planning and preparing meals, using various cooking methods such as baking, frying, grilling, and boiling.

H.E.-HOUSEKEEPING

(Home Economics-Housekeeping)

The H.E. (Home Economics) Housekeeping strand is a technical-vocational track offered in Senior High School in the Philippines. This strand focuses on providing students with the knowledge and skills needed to manage and maintain households, hotels, and other similar establishments. The Housekeeping strand is designed to develop students' competencies in the areas of housekeeping, food and beverage, laundry, and front office services.
