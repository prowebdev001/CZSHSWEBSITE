ACADEMIC TRACK

HUMSS

(Humanities and Social Sciences)

HUMSS stands for Humanities and Social Sciences Strand, which is a senior high school track offered in the Philippines. It is one of the four tracks in the Academic Track, which also includes the STEM, ABM, and General Academic strands. HUMSS is designed for students who are interested in pursuing careers in fields related to social sciences, humanities, and communication, such as education, law, journalism, psychology, sociology, history, and political science, among others. It aims to provide students with a strong foundation in critical thinking, research, communication, and social awareness. The HUMSS curriculum includes subjects such as Philippine Politics and Governance, Creative Writing, Introduction to World Religions and Belief Systems, Disciplines and Ideas in the Social Sciences, and Understanding Culture, Society, and Politics, among others.
